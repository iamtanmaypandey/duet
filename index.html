Program Inclusions

Your PCOS Power Pack

Nutritional Navigator: Discover a personalized nutrition plan tailored to your unique PCOS needs. Say goodbye to guesswork and hello to delicious, PCOS-friendly meals that will help you regain control over your health.

Personalized Fitness Boost : Get one-on-one guidance with a certified personal trainer who understands the challenges of managing PCOS. Exercise is your ally in this journey, and we'll make sure it fits seamlessly into your busy life.

Gynaecologist's Insight : Gain access to expert gynaecological consultations to address your specific concerns and questions about PCOS. Get the medical expertise you need to make informed decisions.

Premium Duuet App : Experience the full power of the Duuet app's premium version, designed to track and manage your PCOS symptoms with ease. It's your personal companion on the road to recovery.

Community Connection : Join our exclusive PCOS community, where you can share stories, advice, and support with others who understand your journey. Forge meaningful connections and celebrate victories together.

Discounts on Health : Enjoy exclusive discounts on essential tests and medications, helping you manage PCOS without breaking the bank. We believe that access to healthcare should be affordable and convenient.

How Duuet Works

Patient Profile Precision


Personal PCOS Portrait

We craft a personalized PCOS profile by delving into your history, lifestyle, career, diet, fitness, stress levels, and habits. Your unique journey begins here.

Gynaecologist's Roadmap


Doctor's Diagnosis

Our experienced gynaecologist examines your physical condition, determining your customized PCOS roadmap and selecting the perfect recovery program for you.

Nutrition & Fitness Tailoring


Nourishment & Fitness Fusion

Our diet and lifestyle coaches collaborate with you, discussing your nutritional and fitness requirements to create a personalized plan that aligns with your PCOS journey.

Duuet App Assistance


Your PCOS Companion

The Duuet app serves as your PCOS assistant, managing your daily needs and sending valuable data to your healthcare team for continuous support.

Data-Driven Adjustments


Smart PCOS Insights

The app feeds your progress data back to your doctor, nutritionist, and lifestyle coaches, who make necessary adjustments to keep your journey on track.

Progress Monitoring & Support


Your PCOS Journey Tracker
